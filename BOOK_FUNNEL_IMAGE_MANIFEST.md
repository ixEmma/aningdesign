## Final image-generation prompts

All article images used the built-in image-generation workflow and were then cropped and compressed to 1200×630 WebP.

### Website Launch Checklist

Premium dark editorial workspace during final website launch review. One designer checks an organized launch board beside a laptop on a charcoal desk. Show responsive design, forms, SEO, performance, analytics, and backups through non-readable checklist blocks. Cinematic elevated three-quarter angle, professional soft light, controlled green accents. No readable text, fake UI, logos, watermarks, floating icons, front-facing screens, clutter, or distorted hands.

### WordPress Website Checklist

Structured website workflow review on a dark designer desk. Arrange six cards, wireframe sketches, a laptop, and a hand placing the final card to suggest planning, design, development, testing, launch, and handover. Ultra-realistic top-down editorial photography, warm neutral paper, restrained green accents. No readable text, logos, fake UI, floating icons, clutter, or distorted hands.

### Website Design Checklist for Clients

Small-business owner preparing organized brand assets and website project materials on a refined charcoal meeting desk: color swatches, logo-shape printouts without branding, image contact sheets, page-list cards, notebook, and an angled laptop. Candid elevated three-quarter editorial photograph. No readable text, fake logos, watermarks, front-facing screens, stock-photo poses, or distorted anatomy.

### WordPress Website Redesign

Professional designer auditing an old website structure and mapping a cleaner responsive replacement. Two angled monitors naturally compare a dense outdated layout and a clean responsive layout, with sitemap sketches and redirect cards on a charcoal desk. Over-the-shoulder editorial photography with controlled green accents. Screens use only non-readable blocks; no logos, labels, watermarks, cyberpunk lighting, or clutter.

### WordPress Launch Checklist

Technical launch-control desk with laptop, backup drive, phone, security key, and organized checklist reviewed by one designer. Visual cues for backups, forms, caching, SEO, security, redirects, mobile testing, and handover. Cinematic elevated side angle, restrained green lights, precise professional mood. No readable text, fake UI, logos, watermarks, WordPress marks, floating icons, or distorted hands.
